# 🛠 Engineering & Research Projects

A selection of projects showcasing my work in **finite element simulation**, **computational mechanics**, and **R&D validation**. These span across **industry experience** and **PhD research** at ETH Zurich.

---

## 🚗 Automotive Structural Testing & Simulation  
**Company:** Leading Automotive OEM (India)  
**Role:** R&D Engineer – Structural Testing  
**Tools:** Abaqus, HyperMesh, Python, MATLAB  
**Summary:**  
Developed FE models and performed static & dynamic validation of chassis and body components. Coordinated with testing teams for correlating CAE results with physical data.

**Highlights:**  
- Improved correlation of simulation and test data by **18%**  
- Reduced design validation cycle by **20%** through predictive modeling  
- Introduced semi-automated post-processing using Python scripting

## 🧱 Master’s Thesis – Nonlinear Simulation of RC Beams  
**Institution:** TU Munich (Master in Computational Engineering)  
**Tools:** Abaqus, UMAT, Fortran  
**Summary:**  
Simulated reinforced concrete beams under shear loading with nonlinear material models. Wrote custom user material subroutines (UMAT) for tension stiffening effects.

**Highlights:**  
- Achieved >90% accuracy compared to test data  
- Demonstrated the role of mesh sensitivity in crack localization  
- Presented at DFG-funded civil engineering workshop

## 📈 Data-Driven Damage Detection in Structures (Side Project)  
**Tools:** Python, NumPy, SciPy, Scikit-learn  
**Summary:**  
Developed a lightweight tool to analyze vibration signals and detect structural anomalies using frequency domain features and machine learning.

**Highlights:**  
- Achieved 92% detection accuracy on benchmark dataset  
- Visualization using PCA + t-SNE for clustering failure patterns  
- Built as an internal tool for R&D validation team
